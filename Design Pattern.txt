Design Patterns are well proven solutions for commonly occuring problems in software devlopment

Categories of design patterns:

1. Creational Design patterns
- Design patterns which provide an approach for creating objects
- Ex: Singleton Pattern, Builder Pattern, Factory Pattern, etc.

2. Structural Design Pattern
- These design patterns provide an approch to strucure various classes/ objects and overall code.
- Ex: Proxy Pattern, Adaprior Pattern, eyc

3. Behavorial Design Pattern
- They provide an approach for interaction between any two objects along with loose coupling.
- Ex: Observer Patter, State Pattern, Iterator Pattern
